## Best Practices

<Tip>
**CV/Resume guidelines:**

1. **Length**: 1-2 pages for industry, unlimited for academic CVs
2. **Font**: Use professional fonts (10-12pt)
3. **Consistency**: Maintain uniform formatting throughout
4. **Keywords**: Include relevant industry keywords for ATS
5. **Quantify**: Use numbers to demonstrate impact
6. **Proofread**: Check for typos and formatting issues
7. **PDF**: Always export to PDF for submission
8. **Tailoring**: Customize for each application
</Tip>

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

<Warning>
**CV/Resume pitfalls:**

1. **Over-designing**: Too many colors, fonts, or graphics
2. **Dense text**: Use white space effectively
3. **Generic content**: Tailor to specific positions
4. **Outdated info**: Keep content current
5. **Missing keywords**: Research job requirements
6. **Poor ATS compatibility**: Test with ATS checkers
7. **Inconsistent formatting**: Use templates
</Warning>
